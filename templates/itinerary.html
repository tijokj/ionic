IIFA Utsavam : Itinerary

First Edition

December 2015 will witness IIFA, India's biggest celebration of Cinema, celebrating South Indian Cinema like never before. Marking a landmark event in the illustrious history of the Cinema of South India, The International Indian Film Academy (IIFA) has announced IIFA UTSAVAM - a magnificent extravaganza envisioned to honor and recognize the best of South India's film industry. Spanning across three days, IIFA Utsavam will debut on the 4th, 5th & 6th of December, 2015 at Gachibowli Outdoor Stadium, Hyderabad.

Day 1 of the event will see IIFA partnering with FICCI for the FICCI-IIFA Business Forum. The forum will feature prominent film personalities, CEOs, policy makers and industry leaders meet to discuss Cinema-tourism, infrastructure, media, cinema co-productions and investment opportunities.

On Day 2, IIFA Utsavam will felicitate and celebrate talent from Tamil & Malayalam film industries.

On the final day, IIFA Utsavam will felicitate and celebrate the best talent from Kannada and Telugu cinema. Nominees be vying for 12 popular awards; 6 special awards will be presented to winners from each region. Common across the 4 language-cinemas, technical Awards will be presented for 10 categories.
